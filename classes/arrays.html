Arrays

An array is a structure that resembles a numbered list. In JavaScript, we treat it like a variable.
Let's say we're listing the names of our friends:

  1. Bowser
  2. Luigi
  3. Yoshi
  4. Mario
  5. Wario
  6. Donkey Kong
  7. Rosalina
  8. Toad
  9. Waluigi

Note that we started at 0 , and that the length of the array is 8 + 1 = 9 .
